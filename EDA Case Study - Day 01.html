Summary - Findings from the Data¶

  • Class 1 passengers seem to have higher prob of Survival.
  • People who perished at 15th April 1912 at 3am came from Southampton...
  • The People who paid more have had a higher survival probability
  • Max Passengers belonged to the Class 3 and this has a higher Rate of Death unfortunately...
  • Females and Children have a higher Survival Rate.
  • We also saw that people are travelling with lot of family members and most likely their survival is a paradox.
  • An interesting pattern that we found is a passenger of 80 years survived..
  • Cabins have a very low set of numbers available
